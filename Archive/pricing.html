Say hello to Cashbook

Cashbook is an open and extensible platform built specifically for Credit Unions, enabling them to integrate PFM data into more member communication channels - automatically helping to drive PFM adoption and providing a competive advantage over traditional banking solutions.

Statements 2.0

Generate member statements with meaning. Provide members with counts and totals for different transaction types, such as retail point of sale and online bill payments, and group transactions and totals between merchants.

Spending Reports

Help members visualize their spending habits over time with transaction summaries categorized for each merchant and bill. Append the report to their monthly statements and provide them with an additional resource for better money management.

Payment Reminders

Predict upcoming payments by grouping related transactions together and computing charge frequency. Sending these reminders out weekly via email or SMS helps members stay on top of their financial obligations and minimize the risk of reduced cashflow.

Internal Analytics

Build an analytics dashboard for internal staff to view member spending habits in real time. By analyzing long term spending behaviours of your members, you gain insight into which financial tools and products will make the greatest net impact in your members financial well being.

Get up and running quicker with pre-built apps.




Budget

A simple budgeting app that lets your members create custom expense categories, set monthly spending targets and receive real-time feedback on the status of their budget via email or SMS.

Patterns

A smart analytics app that gives insight into purchase frequencies and totals spent per merchant over time, helping members understand when, where, how much and how often they spend.

Forecast

An automated forecasting app that analyzes past transactions and provides members with an accurate cashflow projection for the next 4 weeks, helping them keep up with their payments.

Common Questions & Answers

Why use Cashbook?

The Cashbook suite of apps empowers credit union members to make better descisions with their money, creating more savings, less defaults and increasing assets for your organization.

How easy is implementation?

Implementing Cashbook into your credit union infrastructure is a simple two step process consisting of configuring Pennyminder Auth and writing software to supply transactional data.

How secure is member data?

In addition to providing you with an isolated app instance, we also anonymize member data. No names or accounts are stored on our servers, just transactional amounts, dates and descriptions.

How customizable are the apps?

You get a variety of integration points out of the box, including full css control, customizable logo and navigation contents. And if you're really ambitious, you can customize the entire interface.

What hosting options are available?

As a standard, we provide fully managed hosting for both U.S and Canadian credit unions. You also have the option to host one or all of our applications on your own private servers.

Is custom development possible?

Absolutely. If you require any app customization that isn't available out of the box, we'd be more than happy to assist you with development support. Contact us for additional details.
