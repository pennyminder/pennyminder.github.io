Imagine the possibilities...

Reaching more members in more ways leads to increased financial literacy, better spending habits and reduced risk for your financial institution.


Statements 2.0

Generate member statements with meaning. Provide members with counts and totals for different transaction types, such as retail point of sale and online bill payments, and group transactions and totals between merchants.

Spending Reports

Help members visualize their spending habits over time with transaction summaries categorized for each merchant and bill. Append the report to their monthly statements and provide them with an additional resource for better money management.

Payment Reminders

Predict upcoming payments by grouping related transactions together and computing charge frequency. Sending these reminders out weekly via email or SMS helps members stay on top of their financial obligations and minimize the risk of reduced cashflow.

Internal Analytics

Build an analytics dashboard for internal staff to view member spending habits in real time. By analyzing long term spending behaviours of your members, you gain insight into which financial tools and products will make the greatest net impact in your members financial well being.
