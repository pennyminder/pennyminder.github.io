Our Vision

We believe credit unions are the most sustainable, responsible way to bank, and we're dedicated to helping you succeed by providing tools and solutions to empower your members to manage their finances more easily and to make better choices about their finances.

We're a different kind of enterprise software company. We don't charge licensing fees or require long-term support contracts. We just build great products and we deliver them with great service at a reasonable price. We know how important your members are to you; we feel the same way about our clients. Contact us at any time with problems or questions and we'll do our very best to make you happy so you can continue to make your members happy.

Meet the team

Vince Hodges Founder and Principal

Vince is a business minded developer who has been developing software for 30 years. He started Pennyminder to help banks and credit unions help people understand their finances better. Prior to staring Pennyminder, Vince worked in a number of different industries ranging from Gas & Oil to music sharing which gives him the broad experience base to architect and build world class solutions.

In the past, Vince and John worked together at TVI Interactive Systems where they both worked with Vancity to build the Member Direct online banking system, which is now used by over 220 credit unions across Canada.

E. John Love - Product Manager

Before working with Pennyminder, John was active for over 20 years as an all-round creative person for numerous high-tech companies. He's been an art director, gui designer, graphic artist, web designer and technical writer. He's contributed to marketing campaigns, designed user-centred design strategies, and refined software test plans. He loves keeping one foot in the arts and the other one in technology.

John has a diploma in Fine Arts from Emily Carr University, and continues to take an active interest in art, culture and new media. He blogs and tweets compulsively on a wide variety of subjects.
